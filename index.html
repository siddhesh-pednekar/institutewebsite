Shivajirao S. Jondhale College of Engineering (SSJCOE), is an engineering college, located in Dombivli in Thane, Maharastra.
SSJCOE is a private engineering college founded in 1994-95. The college has six departments: Computer Engineering,
Information technology, Chemical Engineering, Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering,
Mechanical Engineering and Production Engineering.
